1.Jampore Beach


Jampore Beach | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Bored of the same old beaches of Goa? Then plan a trip to the charming city of Daman which boasts of virgin palm-lined beaches. Jampore Beach is one such pristine beach in the city which is one of the best places to visit in Daman, known for numerous adventure activities, enthralling views of the expansive Arabian Sea, and a serene environment to give you a break from the bustling life of the city.
  • Location:Jampore Beach Road, Daman
  • Things To Do At Jampore Beach: Parasailing. car racing, camel/ horse ride, beach sports, and relaxing in the shacks
  • Time Required:1-2 hours
  • How To Reach:Take a cab from Naini Damin taxi stand
  • What’s Special:Walk
  • Entry Fee:Free

2.Devka Beach


Devka Beach | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Devka Beach is another popular beach in the town which is known for its long shoreline, fun-filled activities, and a tranquil environment. It is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Daman which lures numerous tourists every year to soak in the tropical weather while relaxing on the beach beds, and enjoy luscious food from the popular shacks lined across the beach.
  • Location:Devka Beach Road, Daman
  • Things To Do At Devka Beach: Chug in exotic drinks while relaxing on the beach beds, savor scrumptious food from the nearby joints, indulge in sporting activities, and visit the nearby amusement park
  • Time Required:1-2 hours
  • How To Reach:Take a cab from Naini Damin taxi stand
  • What’s Special: Adventure sports, horse ridesm
  • Entry Fee:Free

3.Moti Daman Fort


Moti Daman Fort | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Constructed by the Portuguese, towards the end of sixteenth century, the Moti Daman Fort is one of the most popular monuments in Daman. Even though the fort has worn out, and needs restoration, it is still one of the best places to visit in Daman which boasts of Colonial style architecture featuring huge walls, 10 bastions, 2 gateways, and an Iberian church.
  • Location:Moti Daman, Daman and Diu
  • Timings:8 am to 4 pm
  • Entry Fee: INR 10 to INR 15 per person

4.Lighthouse


Lighthouse | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Wondering what to see in Daman? Then plan a visit to the colossal Lighthouse in Daman. Located in the vicinity of the Moti Daman Fort, Lighthouse is one of the most popular Daman tourist attractions. A large number of tourists flock to this place to spectate the marvelous sight of sunset, witness the panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, and the city. It is also a perfect place for photography lovers.
  • Location:Rua Martim Afonso, Fort Area, Moti Daman, Daman
  • Time Required: 1-2 hours
  • How To Reach: Take a cab from Modi Daman
  • What’s Special:Taking pictures during sunrise, long walls
  • Entry Fee:Free

5. Mirasol Lake Garden


Mirasol Lake Garden | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Looking for peace during your trip to Daman? Then Mirasol Lake Garden is the places to go. Blanketed by a green carpet, and numerous trees, this garden is one of the best places to visit in Daman to indulge in an unending affair with nature. The calmness and serenity of the place provide you an opportunity to spend quality time with your loved ones and engage in fun-filled activities for the purpose of entertainment.
  • Location:Near Kadaiya Village, Nani Daman
  • Timings:10 am to 6 pm
  • Entry Fee:INR 10 for adults & INR 5 for children
  • Things To Do: Boating, and enjoying fun filled rides in the amusement area of the park
  • Time Required: 1 hour.
  • How To Reach:Bus to Mirosal water park.
  • What’s Special: Boat rides, beautiful lake.
  • Entry Fee: INR 30, kids – free.

6. Hanuman Temple


Hanuman Temple | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

As the name suggests, this temple is dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It is one of the best places to visit in Daman and Diu if you are looking for religious places during your tour to Daman. Devotees visit this temple to seek knowledge, wealth, relief from diseases, and attain salvation. Located in a peaceful area, this temple also attracts a large number of spiritual seekers who flock here for meditation.
  • Location:Diu, Daman and Diu
  • Time Required:20-30 mins
  • How To Reach:Local transport from Ghanchiwad
  • What’s Special:Medication.
  • Entry Fee:Free.

7.Cathedral Of Bom Jesus


Cathedral Of Bom Jesus | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Due to a strong connection with Portuguese, Daman is populated with Catholics which makes Cathedral of Bom Jesus one of the top Daman tourist places. The construction of this cathedral can be dated back to 1603 and still the building stands strong. The beautiful altarpiece along with the intricately carved doorway has attracted several tourists till now. One must visit this tourist place in Daman to witness the craftsmanship and architecture of this place.
  • Location:Manguerial Rd, Fort Area, Moti Daman, Daman, Daman and Diu 396220
  • Timings:7 am – 9 pm
  • Time Required: 1 hour
  • How To Reach:10kms from Panjim tax stand, local cabs available
  • Entry Fee: Free

8.Nani Daman Fort


Nani Daman Fort | Among the Best Tourist Places in Daman

Nani Daman Fort is one of the most amazing tourist places in Daman. Also popular as the St. Jerome fort, it is a small fort with massive walls made up of stone. Situated on the bank of river Daman Ganga, the fort has an area of 12,250 sq. meters. The fort depicts the Christian culture in many ways with a church, Our Lady of the Sea, in the vicinity and a huge statue of St. Jerome at the fort’s entrance. Some other attractions near the fort include the Gandhi Park and a fish market with several fishing boats at the shore.
  • Location:Near, Nani Daman, jetty, Daman and Diu 396210
  • Timing:8 am – 6 pm
  • Time Required:20-30 mins
  • How To Reach:Can be reached using a boat to Jetty Point, Daman.
  • What’s Special:Jain temples, Statue of Unity.
  • Entry Fee:INR 10
