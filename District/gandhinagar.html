1.Adalaj Stepwell


Adalaj Stepwell | Among the Best Tourist Places in Gandhinagar

Stepwells were introduced in the Western semi-arid regions of India during the 15th and 16th centuries to combat seasonal changes and to support agriculture throughout the year. The Adalaj Stepwell holds a position of significance because of its history and architecture. It is believed that the project for the stepwell was undertaken by a Vaghela ruler named Rana Veer Singh for the welfare of his subjects. Adalaj Stepwell exhibits a marvelous Solanki style of architecture and is five stories deep. This octagonal structure was supported by intricately inscribed pillars, which still serves as a significant source of tourist attractions and is one of the most visited tourist places in Gandhinagar.
  • Location :Adalaj Road, Gandhi Nagar
  • Timings : 24 hours open
  • Best Time to visit : Daytime

3.Akshardham Temple


Akshardham Temple | Among the Best Tourist Places in Gandhinagar

This temple holds the honour of being the most beautiful Akshardham temple of India, and rightly so. Almost two million devotees from all over Gujarat flock to this temple to pay homage to Lord Swaminarayan. The temple is a masterpiece when it comes to an amalgamation of contemporary style while keeping the essence of Hindu mythology alive in the architecture. The structure stands tall in the centre of the 23-acre lawns and has been built using six thousand tonnes of pink sandstone, without the use of steel. Inside the temple, 1.2-tonne gold-plated idol of Lord Swaminarayan is seated with the right hand of the idol raised in Abhay mudra. It is one of those places which offer you much more than the enlightenment that you seek in a temple. Mystic India, a film that takes you through the journey of an 11-year old child yogi called Neelkanth is educative and reflective. A light and music show called Sat Chit Anand Water show and audio-animatronics shows are also held at the temple, which is quite entertaining.
  • Location: 26 km from Ahmedabad
  • Special mention: the Seven-foot high golden idol of Lord Swaminarayan
  • Opening hours: 9:15 AM to 5:15 PM. Mondays closed.
  • Entry Fees: Free

4.Indroda Nature Park


Indroda Nature Park | Among the Best Tourist Places in Gandhinagar

This is one of the fascinating places to visit in Gandhinagar. Often known as the Jurassic Park of India, this place is a favorite among tourists, especially children. Indroda Park is known to be the only park in India which houses fossils of dinosaurs along with a dinosaur museum. Not only that, the park comprises a zoo, an amphitheater and event centers for conducting academic research. If you are a nature lover, the variety of animals and the botanical garden located within the park is sure to attract you. The dinosaur museum is the most popular spot in the park. Overall, this is a perfect place for a picnic with your family or even for a short outing amidst nature.
  • Location - Sector 7, Gandhinagar
  • Timings - 8 AM to 6 PM from Tuesday to Sunday
  • Entry Fee :RS 30 for adults and RS 15 for children between 5-12 years of age. The park also provides subsidized tickets at RS 8 for students.
  • Best Time to visit - Daytime

5.Puneet van


Puneet van | Among the Best Tourist Places in Gandhinagar

Developed in the year 2005, this is a park in Gandhinagar which is known to house around 3500 species of plants. One of the distinct features of this park is that the garden is arranged according to the various zodiac signs in Hindu Mythology. The park is divided into various sections like the panchvati van, the nakshatra van and so on. Each of these sections resembles multiple features from the Hindu Mythology and also depicts what the zodiac signs mean through the arrangement of the trees. Puneet van also links the zodiac signs with the positioning of celestial bodies.
  • Location : Road Number 4B, Sector 18, Gandhinagar
  • Timings :Daytime on all days
  • Best Time to visit :Throughout the year

6.Vithalbhai Patel Bhawan


Vithalbhai Patel Bhawan | Among the Best Tourist Places in Gandhinagar

This building, famous for its architecture holds a position of political importance in Gandhinagar. This is the building where the legislative assembly of Gujarat is located. Thus, one needs special permissions in order to enter the building. Located in new sachivalaya in Gandhinagar, this building was named after Vithalbhai patel, the brother of Sardar Vallabhai Patel. The elaborate architecture with the Ashok Chakra Stambh on the top of the building has become a major tourist spot apart from being an important political centre.
  • Location : Campus of New Sachivalaya, Gandhinagar.
  • Timings :Office hours/daytime.
  • Best Time to visit :All seasons

7.Sarita Udhyan


Sarita Udhyan | Among the Best Tourist Places in Gandhinagar

This is one of the most visited tourist spots in Gandhinagar. Commonly used as a picnic spot, this park is perfect for some nature photography. Located by the riverside in Gandhinagar, you would find plenty of peacocks and other migratory birds playing around the park. The lush green patches, along with the hustle-bustle of the birds, provide you with the most peaceful environment, away from the hubbub of the city. You can also visit the park amidst your sightseeing plan to rest for a while in the middle of the hectic day. In case you just want to explore the park, it would take you around an hour to watch everything inside it.
  • Location :Indroda Village Near Sabarmati River, Sector 9, Gandhinagar.
  • Timings : 24 hours
  • Best Time to visit : Winters
